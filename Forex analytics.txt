Forex analytics
	This application can Log in/out to XTB Broker (Where an account must be created). Application will receive data on traded currencies, account status, closed deals and open deals. With these data, the application will do another operations. The app will be able to open a new deal based on the data you get. For open deals it will check the status of deal repeatedly and then decide if it´s good to close deal or not. The closed deals will be archived by the application. The app holds the open currency price in the database for later analysis and evaluation of the business. The system is intended for people who want to try to by Forex trades.
